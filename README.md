## 📁 Featured Portfolio Project

### 🥛 Lait & Carbone: Scope 3 Analysis of the Isigny Sainte-Mère Cooperative
This personal portfolio project analyzes the 2025 DPEF (Extra-Financial Performance Declaration) of the Normandy dairy giant Isigny Sainte-Mère to align operational performance with Scope 3 carbon reduction targets.

* **The Supply Challenge:** Anticipating and modeling the recurring milk collection drop from May to November (driven by early and persistent heat stress on pastures).
* **Data & Modeling:** 
  * Isolated seasonal trends and modeled milk collection fluctuations using **Prophet**.
  * Formulated a short-term strategic roadmap to transition towards supervised machine learning (**XGBoost**) once daily-granularity data pipelines are structured to optimize transport and eliminate empty runs.
* **Impact & Mitigation Recommendations:**
  * **Medium Term:** Deploying solar panels on farm buildings to power cowshed ventilation and misting systems, mitigating heat stress and securing milk yields.
  * **Long Term:** Establishing local collective methanization partnerships to transform agricultural effluents into biogas, powering the cooperative's transport fleet and industrial sites (deep Scope 3 reduction).
